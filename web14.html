Rana Naidu

Rana Naidu is a 2023 Indian action crime drama television series on Netflix created and directed by Karan Anshuman and Suparn Verma.[2] Produced by Sunder Aaron under the banner Locomotive Global Inc.,[3] it is the official adaptation of the 2013 crime TV series, Ray Donovan.[4][5] It stars Rana Daggubati, Venkatesh Daggubati, Suchitra Pillai, Gaurav Chopra and Surveen Chawla in lead roles.[6][7][8] The series released on 10 March 2023, via Netflix9.
